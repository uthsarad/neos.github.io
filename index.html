What is NeOS

Arch power.
Without the chaos.

NeOS makes deliberate choices so you don't have to. Every update is snapshot-tested, every default is considered, and the experience is refined for people switching from Windows.

Snapshot Rolling

Packages are grouped and tested as coherent sets before promotion. Rolling freshness without raw upstream chaos.

KDE Plasma 6 First

A polished KDE Neon–like experience on Arch with refined defaults and familiar workflows.

Stability Model

NeOS owns the stability model end-to-end with explicit decisions and testing.

Rollback Ready

Snapshot gating and rollback expectations baked in. Recovery is quick when issues happen.
